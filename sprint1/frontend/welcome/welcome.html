What is the UTK CSA?

When the registration season comes along for students at UTK, most students would agree that it can be a tedious process searching for which classes they must take. While the university has resources that provides information on whether or not they're on track to graduate, students still find themselves stressing over what classes to take the upcoming semester (if they haven't filled up already).

With the UTK Class Scheduling Assistant, UTK students will save a lot of time (and anxiety!) when registering for classes by having a schedule generated for them based on their degree path. UTK CSA takes into account the core coursework, time conflicts, geographical location, and general difficulty of each class to create balanced workloads every semester.
